NJ Transit Fatalities

In 2023, at least 20 people died after being hit by a NJ Transit train, as reported by state and local media. However one suddenly dies, whether by suicide or accidentally, these events come with little predictability, making them all the more tragic. This visualization shows each of the incidents while also attempting to capture the unforeseeability of when someone's life is taken.

Once the visualization starts, the date will increment day by day before stopping on the day of a death. The map will display the train station closest to the incident, along with the station's train line. Select the play button below to start the visualization. You can also pause and restart the date incrementation.

Data sources: nj.com, Patch, NJ Geographic Information Network (county map, rail stations),
